About This Game

The Oregon Trail is an educational computer game originally developed by Don Rawitsch, Bill Heinemann, and Paul Dillenberger in 1971 and produced by MECC in 1985. You play as a wagon leader guiding a party of settlers from Independence, Missouri, to Oregon's Willamette Valley via the Oregon Trail in 1848.

This is version 1.4. It requires an Apple IIgs, //c+, or 64K ][+ or //e with a compatible drive controller card.

🦬 Travel the trail, hunt for food, and try not to die of dysentery!
